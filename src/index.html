Two Years of Parole Interviews

The New York State Board of Parole is the sole entity with discretionary authority to grant release to eligible individuals incarcerated with DOCCS. It is an independent body responsible for determining parole, setting parole conditions, and revoking parole when conditions have been violated.

Board interviews include answering questions about prior criminal activity, custodial record, program participation, future goals and release plans.

Bewteen September 2021 and September 2023 the Board of Parole of New York State interviewed incarcerated persons eligible for parole.
